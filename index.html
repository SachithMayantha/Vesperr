WES Product Demo

Quick

WES Quick Demo will address your key concerns and needs. One of our representatives will demonstrate the improvements and workflow specifically for your institute and relevant to your establishment. Quick Demo is suitable for IT experts and individuals who have a considerable knowledge about working with CMS, LMS, SMS or any other online platforms. Quick Demo can consume up to 30 Minutes or more depending on your questions.

Comprehensive

WES Comprehensive Demo is geared to absolute freshers to the online environment. We will assign you with one of our product specialist who will illustrate to you everything you need to know about WES. Note, that this demo can take upto or more than 60 minutes depending on your understanding of the product and we will do everything from our side to make sure to keep jargon to a minimum as possible.
